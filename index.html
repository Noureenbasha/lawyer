About us

Law firms hire lawyers, attorneys, support staff and administrative employees to support legal cases for individuals and businesses1.
There are different types of law firms, depending on their size, structure, practice areas, and client.

Some common types:

Sole proprietorship

A law firm with one lawyer who is responsible for all profit, loss and liability.

General partnership

A law firm with two or more lawyers who share ownership, profits and liabilities

Professional corporation

A law firm that issues stock to the lawyers and has limited liability

Limited liability company

A law firm that has members who are not directly liable to creditors or for negligence

Limited liability partnership

A law firm that has partners who are not liable for each other’s debts or misconduct

Family Law

Law firms handle matters related to family law, such as divorce, child custody, adoption, prenuptial agreements, spousal support, and child support.

Employment Law
